If you're already a devoted surfer, you'll find a variety of different waves and conditions along San Diego's 70 miles of open ocean coastline to beacon you to paddle out. If you're a beginner, San Diego is an ideal place to learn to hang ten. You can also simply take in San Diego's surf culture at one of the yearly surf competitions and events.

You can observe amazing beautiful sunsets around a year.

The San Diego Zoo is housing over 3,700 animals of more than 650 species and subspecies. It is one of the largest zoo in the world.

SeaWorld San Diego is an animal theme park, oceanarium, outside aquarium, and marine mammal park.

San Diego has some yacht clubs, a lot of events and competitions. It will encourage you!
